Project Description:
Glomood is an AI-powered therapy platform designed to make mental health support more accessible, stigma-free, and effective for young adults. By integrating artificial intelligence with evidence-based therapeutic techniques, Glomood provides personalized, on-demand emotional support. The platform offers real-time mood tracking, AI-driven therapy sessions, and peer community engagement, ensuring users receive timely and relevant mental health care.

Integration with Polkadot:
Glomood aims to integrate Polkadot‚Äôs decentralized ecosystem to enhance data security, privacy, and interoperability. By leveraging Polkadot‚Äôs parachain architecture and cross-chain communication, Glomood can securely store sensitive mental health data while ensuring transparency and user control over their information. Additionally, smart contracts can facilitate secure therapist-user interactions, reducing barriers to access and increasing trust in digital therapy.

Why We‚Äôre Interested in Building This for Glomood:
Our team is passionate about bridging the gap in mental health accessibility, especially for young adults who often lack affordable and stigma-free support. With our background in AI, neuroscience, and digital health innovation, we see an opportunity to revolutionize therapy through decentralized technology. Partnering with Polkadot aligns with our vision of creating a secure, scalable, and user-empowered mental health ecosystem.
